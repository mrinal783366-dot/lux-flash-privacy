LUX FLASH

Privacy Policy

Simple, offline, and built to stay out of your data.

Effective date: July 2, 2026

01 — Overview

LUX Flash ("the app", "we", "our") is a flashlight and torch utility for Android. This policy explains what information the app does and does not access, why it requests the permissions it does, and how your data is handled.

The short version: LUX Flash works entirely on your device. It does not connect to the internet, does not create an account for you, and does not send anything you do inside the app to us or to anyone else.

02 — Information We Collect

LUX Flash does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or device identifiers to us or any third party. The app has no internet permission and cannot send data off your device.

03 — Permissions We Use

Android requires apps to declare permissions before using certain hardware. Here's exactly what LUX Flash asks for and why:

Permission Purpose
Camera Hardware only Used solely to access the device's flash/torch unit through Android's Camera2 API so the app can turn the light on and off, and adjust brightness where supported. The app never captures, views, records, or stores any photo, video, or camera preview data.
Vibrate Used to provide short haptic feedback (a small vibration) when you tap controls in the app, if you have this feature enabled in Settings.

LUX Flash does not request location, contacts, microphone, storage, or internet-related permissions.

04 — Data Stored on Your Device

The app saves a small set of preferences locally on your phone only, using Android's standard private app storage. This is never uploaded anywhere. It includes things like:

  • Your preferred brightness level
  • Whether haptic feedback and sound are turned on
  • Your selected theme and default flash mode

Uninstalling the app removes this data from your device.

05 — Third-Party Services

LUX Flash does not integrate any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with third parties because no data leaves your device in the first place.

If this ever changes in a future version of the app (for example, if ads are introduced), this policy will be updated in advance of that change, and the update will clearly describe any new data collection.

06 — Children's Privacy

LUX Flash does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect information at all. The app is safe for general audiences.

07 — Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal data at risk in transit or in storage on our end. Data that stays on your device is protected by Android's standard app sandboxing.

08 — Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app's functionality changes. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
